About us

Pressefy is a leading press release distribution house, committed to ensuring your news reaches the right audience with precision and impact. We specialize in delivering your press releases to a vast network of esteemed media outlets, including MSN, Business Insider, Benzinga, APNews, Digital Journal, Street Insider, Newsmax, and over 300 other platforms.

Our mission is to amplify your brand’s narrative, providing a seamless and efficient way to connect with influential journalists and media professionals. At Pressefy, we understand the critical role that timely and accurate communication plays in today’s fast-paced world. That’s why we offer tailored press release distribution services that not only broaden your reach but also ensure that your message is delivered with the utmost professionalism.

With a focus on quality, affordability, and speed, Pressefy is dedicated to helping businesses, organizations, and individuals share their stories with the world. Our expert team is here to support you through every stage of the process, ensuring that your press releases are crafted and distributed to achieve maximum visibility and engagement.

Trust Pressefy to elevate your brand and make your news resonate across the global media landscape.

FREQUENTLY ASKED
QUESTIONS

Why Should I Choose Pressefy for Publishing My Article?

Pressefy provides a seamless way to get your news stories featured on some of the most prominent and influential news platforms. We give you access to a wide network of sites that are often difficult and expensive to reach on your own. Our team of skilled writers produces SEO-optimized content that effectively promotes your brand and engages a broader audience. With Pressefy, you can expect a hassle-free publishing experience, backed by our dedicated team, all at a price that offers excellent value.

What Is the Expected Turnaround Time for Your Service?

Our typical turnaround time is about 7 business days, though this may vary depending on the package you select. Detailed delivery times are provided on our order form.

Writing Packages: Your news story is usually completed within 2-3 days of your order, with revisions typically handled within 24 hours.

Publishing Packages: The entire process, from order placement to final report delivery, generally takes around 5 business days.

If the submitted content does not meet our writing standards, there may be delays. Additionally, opting for Publishing Add-ons can extend the processing time by 1-4 business days, depending on the add-on.

Can I Make Edits to an Article After It's Published?

Once published, changes to the content are subject to the media outlet's policies. Most platforms may not allow edits after publication. It's important to thoroughly review the article we provide and ensure that any content you submit is fully ready for publication.

Do You Utilize AI Tools for Writing Articles?

No, our articles are written by experienced human writers. Pressefy follows a strict policy against using AI platforms like ChatGPT, ensuring that all content is created with a personal touch by our skilled team.

What Are the Image Specifications for Submission?

Given that news outlets often compress and resize images, we recommend submitting high-resolution images without text to maintain the best quality in the final publication.

What Payment Methods Do You Accept?

We accept payments in BTC, ETH, and USDT only. Full payment is required in advance to initiate the distribution process.

What If a Media Outlet Rejects My Content?

If a media outlet rejects or fails to publish the content, we will provide a suitable replacement with similar reach and credibility. Media replacements are subject to availability and will be selected to match similar traffic and pricing.

How Will I Know My Article Was Published?

A live URL or screenshot will be provided as proof of publication once the press release goes live.
